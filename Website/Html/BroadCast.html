About Searchar AI

Searchar AI is an AI-powered safety and rescue platform designed to help find missing people, detect fires, theft, and other emergencies in real-time.

  • 👁️ AI-powered Face Recognition for missing persons
  • 🔥 Fire & Smoke Detection through CCTV
  • 🚨 Theft & suspicious activity alerts
  • 🤝 Community volunteers for quick rescue actions

Join us in building a safer and smarter society. "Every Eye Counts. Every Life Matters."
