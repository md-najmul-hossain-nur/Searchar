User Policy

User Terms & Privacy Policy

These terms explain how your account works, how we protect your data, and what responsibilities you must follow while using SEARCHAR.

Account Requirements

  • Valid full name, mobile number, and email are required.
  • A valid NID/Birth certificate copy and profile photo must be submitted.
  • Your address and date of birth must be accurate and up to date.
  • You must accept all terms before account activation.

Privacy & Data Use

  • We collect identity and contact data only for verification, safety, and communication.
  • Your personal documents are stored with restricted access controls.
  • Public posts can be visible to others based on your post visibility settings.
  • Security logs may be retained to prevent abuse and fraud.

User Responsibilities

  • Do not submit fake reports, false claims, or manipulated media.
  • Do not impersonate another person or authority.
  • Protect your password and account credentials.
  • Respect community standards and avoid harassment/hate content.

Your Rights

  • You may request profile correction for wrong personal information.
  • You may request account deactivation according to platform policy.
  • You may contact support for data-related questions or complaints.

Important Notice

Any fake document, repeated abuse, or harmful behavior can lead to suspension or permanent account removal.
