Volunteer Safety Policy

Volunteer Terms & Privacy

Please read these rules carefully before joining missions. This policy explains eligibility, conduct, data safety, and account actions for violations.

Before You Start

By continuing as a volunteer, you agree to follow operational instructions, protect sensitive information, and support emergency response with honesty and accountability.

Registration Requirements

  • Provide valid identification documents and a clear profile photo.
  • Use active contact details for emergency communication.
  • Set availability and support capability accurately.
  • Account activation starts only after successful verification.

Operational Conduct

  • Follow mission instructions from authorized control panels.
  • Never submit fake mission completion or false proof.
  • Do not misuse alerts, route tools, or emergency resources.
  • Maintain respectful behavior with victims and responders.

Privacy & Data Handling

  • Mission/location data is used only for coordination and safety.
  • Identity information is protected with restricted access control.
  • System logs may be retained for abuse checks and audits.
  • Sensitive victim data must not be shared publicly.

Restrictions & Penalties

  • Unsafe behavior can lead to temporary mission suspension.
  • Repeated policy violations may remove volunteer privileges.
  • Fake identity or fraudulent claims can cause permanent ban.
  • Serious misuse may be escalated for legal review.

Important Notice

Continuing to use this volunteer account means you accept all terms above and agree to maintain lawful, transparent, and safety-first conduct in every mission.
