Terms of Service

Updated April 2026

Accepting the terms

By joining SEARCHAR as a volunteer, you agree to support emergency missions with honesty, discipline, and respect for legal boundaries.

Changes to terms

Volunteer policy may be revised when operational procedures, legal obligations, or safety standards change.

Volunteer eligibility

Valid identity information and reachable contact details are required for mission assignment and emergency coordination.

Mission conduct

Follow authorized instructions during missions. Misuse of tools, fake completion claims, or non-cooperation is prohibited.

Privacy and data

Victim-related information, mission routes, and internal communication must be treated as confidential.

Safety policy

Do not enter unsafe zones without instruction. Personal safety and victim safety must be prioritized in every operation.

Incident reporting

Mission updates and evidence must be truthful and timely. False reporting can result in account suspension.

Volunteer rights

You may update profile information, raise policy concerns, and request account closure according to platform rules.

Liability policy

SEARCHAR facilitates response coordination but does not replace statutory emergency services or legal authority decisions.

Bangladesh legal terms

Platform usage and policy enforcement are interpreted under the applicable laws and regulations of Bangladesh.
