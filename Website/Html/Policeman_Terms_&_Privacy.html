Authority Policy

Police/Authority Terms & Privacy Policy

These terms cover verification of authority accounts, lawful use of platform controls, and privacy responsibilities for official operations.

Official Verification Rules

  • Official email, badge/ID, and station details are mandatory.
  • Appointment/authorization files must be valid and up to date.
  • Registration is approved only after manual verification.
  • Unauthorized use of police profile is strictly prohibited.

Authority Dashboard Responsibility

  • Moderation, report actions, and assignments must be lawful and fair.
  • Do not disclose investigation-sensitive data in public channels.
  • Use enforcement tools only for genuine safety incidents.
  • Audit logs are retained for accountability and abuse prevention.

Data Handling & Privacy

  • User reports and media must be handled with confidentiality.
  • Authority actions may be reviewed for compliance and legal accuracy.
  • Misuse of data access can lead to account suspension and reporting.

Important Notice

Any forged credential, unlawful action, or repeated misuse of moderation powers may trigger immediate account revocation.
