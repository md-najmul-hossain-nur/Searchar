Camera Contributor Policy

Camera Contributor Terms & Privacy Policy

These terms explain how camera contributors submit feeds responsibly, how media is protected, and what rules apply to contributor earnings and account safety.

Contributor Eligibility

  • Verified identity and valid contact details are required.
  • You must own or be authorized to stream from submitted camera sources.
  • Uploaded camera details must be accurate and lawful.
  • Any fake stream or misleading source can cause account removal.

Feed & Content Rules

  • Do not upload manipulated, prerecorded, or unrelated emergency footage.
  • Do not stream private spaces where consent/legal rights are violated.
  • Contributors must avoid content that can endanger victims or responders.
  • Platform may review and moderate feeds for quality and safety.

Privacy & Payment Notes

  • Submitted media and metadata may be retained for verification and audit.
  • Location and stream logs are used for response mapping and abuse checks.
  • Payout/earning eligibility depends on policy compliance and feed validity.

Important Notice

Unauthorized camera access, fake live feeds, or repeated policy violations may result in payout hold, suspension, or permanent contributor ban.
