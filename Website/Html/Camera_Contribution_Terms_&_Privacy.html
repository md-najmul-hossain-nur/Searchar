Terms of Service

Accepting the terms

By joining as a camera contributor, you agree to provide lawful live sources, maintain data integrity, and follow emergency safety policy.

Changes to terms

SEARCHAR may update these terms to improve platform reliability, legal compliance, and contributor safety standards.

Contributor eligibility

You must own the source or have legal permission to stream from submitted camera locations and maintain accurate account information.

Feed authenticity

Prerecorded, manipulated, or misleading streams are prohibited. Any fake source can result in immediate account suspension.

Privacy and retention

Footage metadata, location logs, and submission activity can be retained for audit, abuse prevention, and public safety mapping.

Earnings policy

Payout eligibility depends on valid stream quality, policy compliance, and successful verification review by the platform.

Quality control

Feeds may be reviewed for clarity, continuity, and relevance to emergency contexts. Repeated low-quality abuse can limit visibility.

Access security

Protect camera credentials and account access. Unauthorized sharing of stream tokens is not allowed.

Liability policy

SEARCHAR coordinates data flow for response support. Operational decisions remain under authorized emergency institutions.

Bangladesh legal terms

Usage and dispute interpretation are subject to applicable laws of Bangladesh and lawful directives of competent authorities.
